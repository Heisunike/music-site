About Showley Bright

Bright Ebinum (/'brit/; born June 24th, 1999), known by his initials Surely Bright (stylized as showley bright),is a Nigerian rapper, singer and songwriter. He released a self-titled E.P in 2019. Showley Bright has actually released a lot of songs before he did his E.P launch though. His first song was a team track with DIVINITY 3 CREW in the year 2015......some of his songs has earned several accolades such as No stress, Gentle beast, Calling etc.......

Early life: Bright was born in Guaraka, Niger State. His parents, mr and Mrs EBINUM are from Ukwani, Delta state. He attended harmony international High School, in dancing rehearsals that's where Bright started free styling on dancing beats as a step dancer then, that was how he was launched into the music world. Bright has two brothers and sisters, Vitalis who is an intelligent electrician, Reachel, Sarah and chidedu Ebinum. During Bright's adolescence, he would listen to tracks from NF, Phora, Billie Eilish, Danny Heroz, Limoblaze, Modallas and Krucifix. After his stint at J.S.S (Gov't high school), he delved into music completely while he was impending to enter S.S.S 1 in Favour wealth high school. In Guaraka town There, he performed regularly at hip hop shows and concerts. His impressive rap flows and attitude gained him a first-place finish! at the show tagged ' emergent of an icon' in 2017.

The EP - Unstoppable Wave

In 2019, the Abuja sensational rapper and singer released a ground-breaking EP tagged "Unstoppable Wave" alongside a concert launching the EP where the likes of WinnerWonder, Hector, Krucifix, G-flow, Unike, Mehcee, Alpha and a bunch of other talented artists performed on stage. The EP featured most of his songs such as Gentle beast, Lie, My Life, Let it go, No stress, Numba1 including his first single titled Ginger where he appreciated God for his unending love and faithfulness in his life. Indeed he is an unstoppable and talented artist whose trails will set a valuable path for generations yet unborn.
